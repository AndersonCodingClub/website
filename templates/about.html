About Us

Purpose:

  • Inspires people to innovate and create through code
  • Support each other in competing in hackathons and other coding competitions

Competitions:

  • Compete in hackathons
  • Develop a project and solve a problem over a short period of time
  • Travel around the region and win prizes and merch
  • Experience the exciting fast-paced atmosphere

Meetings and Structure:

  • Several teams of 3-5 within club
  • Occasional full club meetings to stay on track
  • Individual teams determine their own schedules and projects
  • Teams can collaborate to advance innovation and bring in new perspectives
